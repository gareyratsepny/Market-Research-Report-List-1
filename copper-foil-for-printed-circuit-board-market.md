<p><p>The Copper Foil for Printed Circuit Board market comprises two main types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is produced through mechanical processes, offering uniform thickness and high ductility, ideal for applications requiring flexibility. In contrast, Electrolytic Copper Foil is created through an electroplating process, resulting in a finer grain structure suitable for high-performance circuits. Both types play crucial roles in electronics manufacturing, catering to varying demands based on application requirements and performance specifications.</p></p>
