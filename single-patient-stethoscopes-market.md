<p><p>The Single Patient Stethoscopes market is categorized into Manual and Automatic Stethoscopes. Manual stethoscopes rely on traditional acoustic principles, allowing healthcare professionals to listen to patients' heartbeats and lung sounds directly through the device. In contrast, Automatic stethoscopes use advanced technology to amplify sounds and may incorporate digital features for enhanced diagnostics, such as sound recording and analysis. Both types aim to improve hygiene by minimizing cross-contamination risks associated with traditional stethoscopes used on multiple patients.</p></p>
