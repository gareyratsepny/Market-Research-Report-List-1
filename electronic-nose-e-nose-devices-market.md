<p><p>The electronic nose (e-nose) market comprises various sensor types including Metal Oxide Sensors (MOS), Conductive Polymer Sensors (CP), Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM), Surface Acoustic Wave Sensors (SAW), and others. MOS sensors detect volatile compounds through resistance changes, while CP sensors utilize conductivity alterations in polymer materials. QCM sensors measure mass changes at a quartz crystal surface, and SAW sensors respond to mass variations affecting surface acoustic waves. Each sensor type serves diverse applications in food quality, environmental monitoring, and healthcare.</p></p>
