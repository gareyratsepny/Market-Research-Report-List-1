<p><p>The Sensor Signal Conditioner (SSC) ICs market is segmented primarily into resistive and capacitive types. Resistive SSC ICs are used to process signals from resistive sensors, such as thermistors and potentiometers, converting changes in resistance into voltage or current signals for accurate readings. Capacitive SSC ICs, on the other hand, cater to capacitive sensors, such as pressure and proximity sensors, amplifying changes in capacitance. Both types are essential for enhancing sensor performance, ensuring precise measurements in various applications.</p></p>
