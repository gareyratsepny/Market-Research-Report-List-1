<p><strong>The Flame Arresters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Type Flame Arresters</li><li>Wet-Type Flame Arresters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flame arresters market comprises two main types: dry-type and wet-type flame arresters. Dry-type flame arresters utilize a series of metal mesh screens to disrupt flame propagation through gases or vapors, making them suitable for flammable gas applications. Wet-type flame arresters, on the other hand, employ a liquid barrier to cool and extinguish flames, providing enhanced protection in liquid fuel environments. Both types are essential for preventing explosions in various industrial settings, ensuring safety and compliance.</p></p>
