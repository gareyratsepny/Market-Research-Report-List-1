<p><p>The fiber holders market encompasses two primary types: fiber holders for single fibers and fiber holders for fiber ribbons. Single fiber holders are designed to securely hold individual optical fibers, ensuring precise alignment and protection during handling and installation. In contrast, fiber holders for fiber ribbons accommodate multiple fibers grouped together, facilitating efficient management and organization of ribbonized fibers. Both types are crucial for maintaining the integrity and performance of fiber optic networks across various applications.</p></p>
