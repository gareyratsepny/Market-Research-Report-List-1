<p><p>The Self-Checkout POS Terminal market consists of three main types: cash self-checkout terminals, cashless self-checkout terminals, and hybrid checkouts. Cash self-checkout terminals allow customers to pay using physical currency, catering to those who prefer cash transactions. Cashless self-checkout terminals facilitate payments via digital methods like credit cards or mobile wallets, enhancing convenience and speed. Hybrid checkouts combine both options, offering flexibility for customers to choose their preferred payment method, thus accommodating a wider range of consumer preferences.</p></p>
