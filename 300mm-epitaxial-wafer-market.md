<p><p>The 300mm epitaxial wafer market is segmented into P-type and N-type wafers, each serving distinct applications in semiconductor manufacturing. P-type wafers, with holes as majority carriers, are typically used in devices requiring p-n junctions, like diodes and transistors. In contrast, N-type wafers, featuring electrons as majority carriers, are preferred for applications demanding high electron mobility, such as high-performance and power electronics. The choice between these types is crucial for optimizing device performance and efficiency in various electronic applications.</p></p>
