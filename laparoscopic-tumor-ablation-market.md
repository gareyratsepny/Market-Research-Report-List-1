<p><p>The laparoscopic tumor ablation market includes several techniques. Microwave ablation employs electromagnetic waves to generate heat, destroying tumor tissue. Radiofrequency (RF) ablation uses radiofrequency energy to produce heat and induce necrosis. Chemical ablation involves the injection of substances to liquefy and destroy tumors. Cryotherapy freezes and kills cancer cells by applying extreme cold. Other methods may include laser ablation and ultrasound techniques, offering diverse approaches to effectively treat tumors minimally invasively. Each technique is tailored to specific tumor types and patient needs.</p></p>
