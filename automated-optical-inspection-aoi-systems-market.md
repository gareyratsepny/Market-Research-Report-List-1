<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems are essential in the electronics manufacturing industry for ensuring product quality. The market is divided mainly into two types: 2D AOI systems and 3D AOI systems. </p><p>2D AOI systems capture two-dimensional images of circuit boards to detect defects by comparing against predefined standards. In contrast, 3D AOI systems utilize advanced imaging technology to analyze the height and volume of components, offering a more comprehensive inspection, enabling the detection of defects that may be missed by 2D systems.</p></p>
