<p><ul><li>Layered Zirconium Crowns</li><li>Monolithic Zirconium Crowns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The zirconia crowns market is primarily divided into two types: layered zirconium crowns and monolithic zirconium crowns. Layered zirconium crowns feature a core of zirconium oxide covered by a ceramic layer, offering enhanced aesthetics and a natural appearance. In contrast, monolithic zirconium crowns are made entirely of zirconium, providing exceptional durability and strength, making them ideal for high-stress areas. Both types cater to different patient needs, balancing aesthetics and functionality in dental restorations.</p></p>
