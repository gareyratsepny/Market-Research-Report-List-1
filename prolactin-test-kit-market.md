<p><p>The Prolactin Test Kit market comprises various types, notably Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRF utilizes fluorescent markers to detect prolactin levels, offering high sensitivity and rapid results. In contrast, CLIA employs chemiluminescent reactions for quantifying hormone concentrations, known for its accuracy and efficiency in clinical settings. Both methods are vital for diagnosing disorders related to prolactin imbalance, catering to healthcare providers seeking reliable and precise diagnostic tools for patient management.</p></p>
