<p><p>Plastic clamshell packaging is categorized into several types based on the resin used. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) offers flexibility and durability, making it ideal for food packaging. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is widely favored for its recyclability and clarity, suitable for retail products. Polystyrene is lightweight and cost-effective, often used for disposable containers. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) provides high impact resistance, commonly utilized for electronic and consumer goods packaging. Each material balances functionality, cost, and environmental considerations in packaging solutions.</p></p>
