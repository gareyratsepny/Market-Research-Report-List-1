<p><p>Soluble support materials for 3D printing enhance the production of complex geometries by providing temporary structures that can be easily removed after the printing process. PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) is a water-soluble material, ideal for organic and detailed designs, dissolving in water without leaving residue. HIPS (High-Impact Polystyrene) is used with ABS filaments, offering support that can be dissolved in limonene, making it suitable for designs requiring durability. Other types include various proprietary blends optimized for specific applications, balancing solubility, strength, and ease of removal.</p></p>
